Dr. Martin Luther King Jr

"I have a dream"
Dr. King giving a speech
Martin was a crucial figure during the civil rights era. As a priest, he lead the way for blacks at the time, whom were held down by current laws from the Jim Crowe era. Eventually, however, his willingness to push back against the norms lead him to his early and tragic death. He was assasinated outside Memphis, TN. while he was giving a speech. His death however, didn't go in vein. He is someone who is continually admired for the way he not only invisioned the future, but did everything he could to help create that future.
